P1xT Curriculum

Thu Feb 16th 2021 7:55 PM PST

With so many resources online aimed to learn and/or improve programming skills, at times I felt like I was drowning in options. How do I know I am spending my time wisely to efficiently build up my skills? Well, through my daily devouring of Reddit news and articles I came across a user who posted a guide put together with the best free courses available from various sites across the internet. Naturally skeptic, I had to read through a quite a few positive comments from fellow programmers before I decided to make P1xt's guide my course of focus. After a few Khan Academy courses, I am now in the early stages of the Full Stack Course offered through App Academy Open and I love it!!! Learning a new language, Ruby, while freshening up more programming skills every day. Site updates and more projects will start being added once this Full Stack Course is completed.
